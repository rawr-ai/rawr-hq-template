## Preserved Outcome

- Workstream is the primitive bounded execution unit.
- Program, phase, and runtime-specific proof vocabulary must not become generic
  workstream law.
- The reusable shape belongs in skills, references, assets, steward briefs, and
  mechanical hook scripts.
- Runtime Realization Lab material is an overlay: authority order,
  proof/evidence classes, gates, evidence homes, phase dossiers, and migration
  boundaries.
